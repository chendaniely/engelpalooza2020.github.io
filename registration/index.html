If you wish to register for Engelpalooza, please fill out the qualtrics forms here!

Registration for Engelpalooza is free, but required, for both attendees and presenters. You may present a poster or talk that you have given at a different scientific conference or symposium in the last 6 months. Presentations are limited to 8 minutes, and format (as a poster or as a series of slides) is up to you.

To present at Engelpalooza, you must either be: (i) a Virginia Tech undergraduate researcher pursuing a Bachelors degree in biochemistry, (ii) a Virginia Tech undergraduate researcher working in the lab of a current biochemistry faculty member, or (iii) a Virginia Tech graduate researcher pursuing a Masters or Doctoral degree in biochemistry. Registration for presenters closes Sunday, October 25, at midnight. We hope to see you there!
