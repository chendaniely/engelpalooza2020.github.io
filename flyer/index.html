New to Discord?

Discord is a messaging service that is intended to serve as a networking and meeting place for Engelpalooza 2020 attendees to communicate, discuss and share their work, and sync up with conference events. The following instructions were adapted from https://bcc2020.github.io/

Getting Discord:

  • Discord in your browser: https://discord.com/app
  • Discord on your desktop https://discord.com/new/download
  • Discord on your mobile device

We recommend using the Discord application on your desktop instead of the browser version, but either should work fine. Please avoid using the mobile version unless absolutely necessary.

Logging in:

When you follow the invitation link above, you will be invited to join the group with a page that prompts you for a username. Choose a username to log in. Quick Overview:

Once you have access to the server, you will see the list of categories and channels on the left side of the screen, the chat window in the center, and the member list on the right.

  • Channel: a chat window. Can be either text or voice, but usually text. Shown on the left side in the channel list preceded with #.
  • Category: Grouping of channels. Shows on the left side in the channel list as an all caps heading.
  • Channel list: the complete list of channels and categories, shown on the left side of the desktop app.
  • Member list: the listing of members/attendees in the current channel, shown on the right side of the desktop app

Getting Started with Engelpalooza 2020 Discord Server:

You should be in the #check-in-registration when you first access the discord server. Here, you will work with the organizers and student support to check-in. Reminder: registration is required to access the symposium. Once you have checked in, you will have access to the #welcome-and-info channel under Symposium Information. Please read the information there for next steps. Do not forget to read the Code of Conduct (also listed below) as the symposium will be strictly monitored for inappropriate behavior.
